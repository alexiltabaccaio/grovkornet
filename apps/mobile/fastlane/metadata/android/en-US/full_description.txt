Capture the soul of analog photography on your mobile device.

Grovkornet (Swedish for "coarse-grained") is a high-performance camera designed for photographers, content creators, and analog enthusiasts. Unlike standard filter apps that apply flat overlays, Grovkornet features a custom, hardware-accelerated rendering engine built on Google Filament. It simulates the physical path of light through vintage optics, camera sensors, and chemical development in real-time, delivering a stable, silky-smooth 60 FPS viewfinder experience.

Take complete control over your creative vision with our physical-hardware metaphor interface, organized into three tactical blocks:

⚙️ SYSTEM
Quickly manage your preferences, technical overlays, and application settings.

👁️ LENS (Optics Simulation)
Recreate the behavior of classic vintage glass.
• Focus: Tactile, precise manual focus control.
• Chromatic Aberration: Simulate physical glass light dispersion.
• Bloom: Emulate the dreamlike light bleed of vintage lenses.
• Lens Selection: Quickly switch between your device's physical cameras.

📷 BODY (Sensor & Mechanics)
Interact with the camera body just like a professional rig.
• ISO & Shutter Speed: Dial in your exposure manually for the perfect photo.
• Aspect Ratio: Choose from classic 16:9, widescreen 2.39:1 (Cinematic look), square, or social-ready formats.
• Resolution & Torch: Adjust capturing parameters on the fly.

🎞️ FILM (Chemical Development)
Develop your look with an advanced grain and color pipeline.
• Organic Grain: Customize grain size, intensity, and roughness to emulate 8mm, 16mm, or 35mm stocks.
• Color Balance: Adjust Temperature and Tint with precise Kelvin-like response.
• Texture Tuning: Refine Saturation, Contrast, and Sharpening.
• Noise Reduction: Apply hardware-accelerated smoothing before development.

KEY FEATURES:
• Real-time Multi-pass GPU Pipeline (Uber Shader): Zero post-processing lag—what you see is what you capture.
• Raw Performance: Silky-smooth 60 FPS live preview utilizing native C++/Kotlin optimizations.
• Custom Presets System: Save your favorite LENS, BODY, and FILM configurations as custom presets, complete with auto-generated visual thumbnails.
• Dynamic Gallery: Instantly preview and organize your custom shots.
• Privacy First: All processing happens entirely offline on your device.
