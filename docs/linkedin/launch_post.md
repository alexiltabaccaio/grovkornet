## Post 1: Project Announcement (Bilingual)

🇬🇧 **English** (🇮🇹 Italiano scorrendo in basso)

I’m excited to share a major milestone: after 9 years running my tobacconist business, I am officially leaving the retail sector to fully dedicate myself to my true passion: software engineering. 💻

To mark this transition, I’m thrilled to announce a project I’ve been pouring my heart into: **Grovkornet** 🎞️.
It’s a mobile camera ecosystem for real-time cinematic film emulation, but the real challenge was **under the hood**.

To achieve authentic analog looks without draining the battery and maintaining a stable 60 FPS, standard libraries weren't enough. So, I:
🔧 Built a custom native rendering engine from scratch in **C++ and Kotlin** powered by Google Filament.
⚡ Implemented a single-pass GPU pipeline (Uber Shader) that physically emulates the path of light: from the lens, to the sensor, to chemical development.
🏗 Structured the entire project as a Monorepo (Turborepo) strictly adhering to **Feature-Sliced Design (FSD)**.
🕸 Created an in-house AST analyzer ("Code GraphRAG") to validate and protect the architectural boundaries in CI.

From managing a shop to building custom C++ rendering engines — it’s been an incredible learning journey, and I’m just getting started.
