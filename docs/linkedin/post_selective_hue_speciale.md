Today, an off-schedule sneak peek of what I am building with **Grovkornet**.

In this image, you can see the **Selective Hue** feature in action. Disclaimer: what you see on the right **is not the result of photo editing software** applied after the fact, but it's **exactly what you see in real-time in the viewfinder** before even pressing the shutter button.

Building a custom rendering engine as a solo developer has been a massive challenge, but the results are incredibly rewarding. Under the hood, this is powered by my custom engine optimized to process **hardware-accelerated 3D LUTs (Look-Up Tables)** live at 60 FPS. This allowed me to render a perfectly autumnal picture even though I was shooting in the middle of summer. 

The goal? Giving creators total freedom to define their photographic vision the exact moment they frame the shot.
