🚀 Release: Version 0.11.1 (First Closed Beta) @everyone - The first test build is officially rolling out to approved testers via Google Play!

This is our baseline build. It includes the foundation of the NativeFilmCamera engine and the core UI. Here is what you can test right now:

✨ What's Included:

🔹 Custom Native Engine: Live viewport rendering at 60fps (powered by C++/Kotlin and Google Filament).
🔹 Core Camera Controls: Manual focus, ISO, Shutter Speed, and Exposure compensation.
🔹 Film Simulation Pipeline:
   • Dynamic Film Grain (adjustable intensity, size, and roughness).
   • Chromatic Aberration & Lens Distortion.
   • Custom Bloom effects.
   • Selective HSL color tuning & White Balance tint.
   • Retro CRT scanlines overlay.
🔹 UI & Experience: Clean, distraction-free interface built to feel like a real camera, with haptic feedback integration.

🐛 Known Issues & What to Look For: Since this is our first real-world test, we are mainly looking for device-specific bugs. Please keep an eye out for:

🔸 Viewport freezing or lagging on older Android devices.
🔸 Unexpected crashes when rapidly changing parameters (like grain intensity or bloom).
🔸 UI elements not scaling correctly on different screen sizes.

Found a bug? Have an idea? Please drop it in #bug-reports 🛠️ or just chat about it in #general 💬!

Have fun shooting! 📸
