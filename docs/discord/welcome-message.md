Hey everyone! Thank you for joining the community. 🎉

Grovkornet (Swedish for "coarse-grained") is a high-performance analog film camera emulator for Android. Unlike standard filter apps, it features a custom C++/Kotlin native rendering engine based on Google Filament, simulating the physical path of light through a camera setup in real-time at a stable 60 FPS viewport.

As a tester, you will get early access to try and tweak our physical camera simulation across its modular layers:
📸 LENS: Optical adjustments and focus controls.
⚙️ BODY: ISP mechanics (manual ISO, shutter speed, exposure, and aspect ratio controls).
🎞️ FILM: Chemical development emulation, including selective HSL color tuning, white balance tint, custom bloom, chromatic aberration, scanlines, and an ultra-realistic film grain simulator (intensity, size, speed, and roughness).
